About Us:

Do you ever look at fish and think: "You should be a cookie."? Well us over here at Salmon Cookie Shop think the exact same thing! We are a family owned desert shop. We currently have 5 different locations for your salmon cookie convenience! All of our cookies are made to give you that special Salmon Cookie feeling! All of our stores are open from 10 AM to 9Pm, 7 days a week.
